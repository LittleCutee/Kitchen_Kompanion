Salmon

Step 1: Ingredients preparation: salmon, olive oil, lemon, spray cooking oil.

Step 2: Pre-heat oven to 450°F with your baking sheet in the oven.

Step 3: Brush (all sides of the) salmon with oil, and season with salt & pepper.

Step 4: Place in the pan skin side down and cook for 3 minutes.

Step 5: Carefully flip and cook for another 2-4 minutes, until fish is cooked through to 125°F and flakes with a fork.

Shrimp Sausage

Step 1: Ingredients preparation: 13 ounce bag shrimp or crab boil, 3 tablespoons Old Bay Seasoning, 2 pounds small new potatoes, 4 corn, husked and cut in 1-1/2- to 3-inch pieces, 3 lemons, halved, Snipped fresh Italian (flat-leaf) parsley

Step 2: In a 12- to 16-quart pot bring 2 gallons water to boiling. Add shrimp or crab boil and Old Bay Seasoning.

Step 3: Transfer to large platter. Add lemon halves and sprinkle parsley.

Step 4: Serve hot or within 1 hour of cooking.

Step 5: Pass Garlic Mayonnaise and Cocktail Sauce. Makes 6 to 8 servings.

Teriyaki Chicken

Step 1: Ingredients preparation: chicken, salt and pepper, olive oil, soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic grove

Step 2: Cook Chicken: Cut the chicken into one inch pieces and salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in a wok or skillet over medium high heat and add the chicken. Cook until no longer pink and remove and set aside on a plate.

Step 3: Whisk: In a small bowl combine the soy sauce, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, garlic, sesame oil, and ginger. Then, in a separate small bowl whisk the water and cornstarch to make a slurry. Add the cornstarch to the sauce mixture and whisk.

Step 4: Thicken Sauce: Add the sauce to the skillet and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and then whisk until it starts to thicken.

Step 5: Toss Chicken with Sauce: Add the chicken back to the skillet and toss until coated and heated through. Garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds. Serve over rice if desired.
